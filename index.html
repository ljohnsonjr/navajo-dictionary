          About Diné Bizaad Dictionary

          This dictionary contains over 8,600 Navajo words and phrases to help learners, speakers, and researchers explore the richness of the Diné language.

          The Navajo Alphabet

          Navajo uses the Latin alphabet with several additions. Special characters include high-tone vowels (á, é, í, ó), nasal vowels (ą, ę, į, ǫ), and the lateral fricative ł. The glottal stop ʼ is also a distinct sound.

          How to Use

          • Search — Type any Navajo or English word. Diacritics are optional; searching "hozho" will find "hózhó."
          • Browse — Tap a letter to see all words starting with it.
          • Favorites — Tap the heart icon to save words for later. Favorites are stored in your browser.
